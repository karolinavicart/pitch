What this calculator does*
It estimates how much recurring, manual Coupa/AP work could be automated by:
– converting hours per month into FTEs, and
– multiplying those FTEs by a loaded annual cost.

Use the “Automatable hours” fields only for tasks that are realistically automatable (e.g. GR chasing, coding checks, supplier follow-up), not for the entire AP job.
